# Your Name in Landsat Changelog

## [Initial Version] - 2026-05-13

- Generate Landsat satellite imagery from a name (up to 16 letters)
- Detail view with tile metadata and links to NASA sources
- History command to browse, view, copy, paste, download, and clear past generations
- Copy image, copy tile links, copy coordinates, paste to active app, download to Downloads folder
- Source imagery by NASA / U.S. Geological Survey (USGS)
